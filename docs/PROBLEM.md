# Problem

Public blockchains expose all financial activity.

Every transaction reveals:

sender address  
receiver address  
amount transferred  
wallet balances  
transaction history

Once a wallet address becomes linked to a real-world identity, the entire financial history becomes public.

Example:

alice.eth → 0xABC123

Anyone can see:

Alice's total holdings  
all payments she received  
all payments she sent  
all DeFi activity

---

## Real World Problems

Freelancers expose income.

Companies expose treasury balances.

DAOs expose governance spending.

Donors expose affiliations.

Traders expose portfolio size.

Financial privacy is a fundamental requirement for real-world adoption.
